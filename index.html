Hello!

Welcome to my portfolio, my name is Javier Alejandro López an art director from Venezuela, now based in Santiago, Chile. I focus on display strategy and brand experiences—from concept to execution. Over the past few years, I’ve led the creative development of retail spaces and product exhibitions for Sony, including the full redesign of the Sony Store and interactive displays across major malls in Chile.

My work blends visual storytelling with strategic thinking to create spaces that not only look great but also connect with people. Whether it’s launching a new product line, designing immersive experiences, or reimagining how a brand shows up in-store, I’m all about bringing ideas to life in a bold and meaningful way.

In this portfolio, you’ll find a curated selection of my recent work, including Display development, Graphic Design, Illustrations, and Experimental Personal Projects that showcase my creativity and range across different mediums.

If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to reach out via WhatsApp or email—I’d love to connect!
