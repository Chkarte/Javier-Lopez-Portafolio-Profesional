Hello!

Welcome to my portfolio, my name is Javier Alejandro López, I am a Venezuelan visual artist based in Santiago de Chile. Since my beginnings as a graphic designer I've been using the tag name of CHK.

This is a brief summary about my most relevant projects at Sony Electronics Chile, where I developed as Visual & Display Supervisor in the Chilean retail market. These projects are part of the visual strategy that I created over the years. In addition, I include different freelance projects that I have carried out in recent times.

I consider myself an enthusiast of graphic communications, I am always looking for creative solutions that directly impact consumer perception.

NEW SONY CORNER
PARIS PLAZA EL TREBOL

Project Direction, Display
×

Nes Sony Corner Paris Plaza El Trebol

A Sony exhibition was made in a dedicated corner of a major shopping mall in a key region of Chile. The space was designed to highlight Sony’s latest technology and product innovations, offering visitors an immersive brand experience. The setup included interactive displays, product demos, and hands-on stations where customers could explore Sony’s audio solutions. The exhibition aimed to strengthen brand presence in the region while allowing consumers to engage directly with the products in a high-traffic, accessible location.

Paris Mall Plaza El trebol (Concepción), Chile 2023
